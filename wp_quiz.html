Win Probability Intuition Quiz

20 real NFL scenarios  ·  ~10 minutes

Thank you for taking the Win Probability Intuition Quiz. The goal is to collect data on how a diverse group of people understand and interpret win probability in the context of American football.

The quiz presents 20 scenarios from NFL games over the last 20 years. For each, you'll be given a freeze-frame of the game state: the score, time remaining, timeouts, and down and distance. You will not be given the teams, the year, the players, or the decision the team made at that moment. Your job is to predict Team A's win probability; Team A is always the team with possession.

Some scenarios may be recognizable. If you think you know the game, try to set that aside and estimate based on the game state in front of you rather than the outcome you remember or think you remember.

There are no trick questions. These 20 scenarios were chosen because they cover a wide range of game states, not to catch anyone out. Thanks for your time and honest answers.

Cam

How It Works

  • You will see 20 real historical NFL game situations, one at a time.
  • Team A is always the team with possession of the ball.
  • Use the slider to estimate Team A's chance of winning (0–100%).
  • Enter the final margin you'd expect — positive if Team A wins, negative if Team B wins.
  • After submitting each answer, you'll see how you compared to the model.
